A loss of protected land
The total acres of land managed under the Conservation Reserve Program in Minnesota has steadily declined since 2007.
Data source: U.S. Department of Agriculture | Graphic by Jeff Hargarten, Star Tribune
Changes to CRP lands
CRP acreage throughout Minnesota has declined since 2007 in most counties – particularly in the Iron Range – and only increased in a dozen areas.
